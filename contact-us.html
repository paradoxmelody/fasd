What We Offer

Available Services

Clinics open Monday – Friday, 08:00 – 16:30

Individual Sessions

One-on-one counselling sessions, with or without family members present, tailored to your recovery journey.

Group Sessions

Skills-focused group work to help you maintain sobriety and build a life free from alcohol and drugs.

Family Education

Dedicated groups that educate you and your family about the recovery process and how to support one another.

Social Support Groups

Regular meetings connecting you with others navigating substance abuse recovery — community-driven and ongoing.

Matrix® Programme

Evidence-based outpatient treatment — three to four sessions per week following the internationally recognised Matrix® model.
