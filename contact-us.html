Organisation

City of Cape Town — City Health Department

Municipal health services providing accessible alcohol and drug outpatient treatment across Cape Town.

Representative: Letitia Bosch

What We Offer

Clinics open Monday – Friday, 08:00 – 16:30

Individual Sessions

One-on-one counselling, with or without family members, tailored to your recovery journey.

Group Sessions

Skills-focused group work to help you maintain sobriety and build a life free from alcohol and drugs.

Family Education

Groups that educate you and your family about the recovery process and how to support one another.

Social Support Groups

Regular meetings connecting you with others navigating recovery — community-driven and ongoing.

Matrix® Programme

Evidence-based outpatient treatment — three to four sessions per week following the internationally recognised Matrix® model.
